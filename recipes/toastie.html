Tasty Ham and Cheese toastie

Making the best Roasties

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of thick bread
  • 30g of your favourite cheese, sliced or grated
  • 2 slices of ham
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonaise
  • [Optional] 1 teaspoon of english mustard

Steps

  1. Make up the ham and cheese toastie as if making a standard sandwich
  2. If you like a bit of heat mix the grated/sliced cheese with the mustard
  3. "Butter" the outside of the sandwich with the mayonaise, this will prevent the bread from cooking too quickly
  4. Place on a grill tray and cook for 4-5 mins per side or until golden brown and crunchy
  5. [Optional] For those who like an extra cheesey toastie, once it is cooked, layer another layer of cheese on the top and put back under the grill until fully melted
  6. Slice into halves and enjoy!
